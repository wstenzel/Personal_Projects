Naperville, IL

ADVENTURE • LEADERSHIP • NATURE • HONOR • FUN

Troop 222: One of most accomplished and established Troops in Naperville. 2023 marks our 35 year.

Troop 9222: Our sister Troop. Launched in 2019 and still going strong.

Our adventures include: canoeing, caving, rock climbing, horesback riding, and more.

We meet every Monday from 6:45-7:45 at Meadow Glens Elementery School, except for one home-base patrol meeting a month.
